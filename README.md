## Project Overview

This project is something I did as part of the Student Digital Ambassador program. The main goal of this project is to help people learn the skills they need to use technology and the internet in a good way. The Student Digital Ambassador program focuses on things like knowing about literacy making a professional online presence, understanding how to communicate properly online and staying safe on the internet.
The project has five parts. Each part is designed to help people learn by actually doing things with modern digital tools. The project also teaches people how to keep a digital identity on websites like Canva, GitHub and HackerRank. The project also includes a case study to teach people about things that can happen online like phishing and other cyber threats.
Overall the Student Digital Ambassador program and this project help people understand what digital literacy is and how to be a digital citizen. The project is really about learning how to use the internet and technology in a way and that is what the Student Digital Ambassador program is all, about.

## Module 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness

I made an infographic, about digital literacy using Canva. This infographic shows students the digital tools they can use and how to stay safe on the internet.

My goal was to make this information easy to understand. I wanted my peers to be able to look at it and use it in their lives when they are online.

## Module 2 – Professional Online Presence

For this module I made profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn and HackerRank. These are the places where I will show who I am online.

My GitHub profile has a page that says who I am, what I like and what I want to learn. I took pictures of all these profiles. I explained how I will use them to learn and get a job someday. I did this so people can see what I can do on GitHub, LinkedIn and HackerRank.

## Module 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms

I worked on coding and collaboration tools in this module. I did a beginner programming challenge on HackerRank. This helped me learn how to solve problems and get better at coding.

I also made a quiz on Google Form about literacy. I wanted to get my friends involved and make learning fun. This showed me that online platforms are really useful for learning and working together.

## Module 4 – Professional Communication & Social Media

This module was about communicating in a professional online setting. I wrote two emails. One was to a professor and the other was to an internship coordinator. I made sure to follow the rules of writing emails.

I made a list of things to do. Not to do on social media. This helped me understand that I need to be professional and responsible when I am online. I learned about Professional Communication and Social Media in this module and I learned about coding and collaboration platforms, in Module 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms.

## Module 5 – Cybercrime Awareness

I looked into a case of cybercrime like when someone tries to trick you with phishing and I made a detailed report about what happened what could go wrong and what might happen if you are not careful.

I also made a list called "Stay Safe Online" that has some tips for students, like how to keep your personal stuff and money safe and what to do if someone tries to commit a cybercrime against you. Doing this module really helped me understand the dangers that're online and what I can do to protect myself from cybercrime and cybercrime threats.

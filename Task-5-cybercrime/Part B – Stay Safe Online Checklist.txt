Part B – Stay Safe Online Checklist

Stay Safe Online – Cyber Safety Checklist for College Students:

1.	Never share your UPI PIN, OTP or bank details with anyone even if they claim to be from your bank.
2.	Remember that receiving money never require entering your UPI PIN. Entering PIN always means you are sending money.
3.	Avoid clicking on unknown links in SMS, emails, or WhatsApp messages.
4.	Only download apps from official app store like Google Play or Apple App Store.
5.	Enable two-factor authentication on important accounts such as email and banking apps.
6.	Do not connect to public Wi-Fi networks when making financial transactions.
7.	Check the UPI payment request details carefully before approving any transaction.
8.	Regularly update your phone software and app to stay protected from security vulnerabilities.
9.	If you suspect fraud, immediately report it to the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in.
10.	You can also call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 to quickly report financial fraud.

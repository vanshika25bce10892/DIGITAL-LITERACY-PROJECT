# Part A - CASE STUDY:



### Cybercrime Type: UPI / Online Payment Fraud



Online payment fraud has become a big problem in India because so many people use UPI apps like Google Pay, PhonePe and Paytm. In this type of crime bad people trick others into sending them money or giving away financial information.

A typical UPI fraud can happen in steps:

1. First the bad person contacts the victim by phone, SMS, WhatsApp message or a fake advertisement. They often say they are from the bank or that they are helping with a problem or that they are buying something from the victim online.

2. Second the bad person sends a payment request or a bad link and asks the victim to say yes to it so they can get the money.. Really the victim is allowing money to be taken from their own account. Sometimes the bad person also asks for the code or PIN saying it is needed to check everything is okay.

People in college older people and people who buy things online a lot are often targeted because the bad people think they may not know how online payments work.

When someone gets tricked like this it can be very bad. They can lose a lot of money away and it can be hard to get it back because the bad people send the money to many different accounts. It is not about losing money the victim can also feel very stressed and not want to use online services anymore.

The Indian National Cyber Crime Portal says that many people report UPI fraud every year. This shows that we need to be more careful and know more, about how to stay online.
